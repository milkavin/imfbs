About Us

'IMFBS' is an online shop focused on selling women outfit since 2013.
It stands for:
- i am fabulous -

We opened the store to support women to express themselves and be fabulous with our clothing line.

First, the store opened and served customer on social media platform. During that time there was a raise of online shop on that platform. We focused on selling clothing line with cheap price but expensive quality and services. As the number of customer increased (based on number of followers and orders), the store opened an offline store if customer wants to visit and purchase on the site.

On 2015, the store decided to spread its wings. Opened an online store on one of e-commerce to gain more customer and branding the store.
Why e-commerce? In e-commerce, there is a trust between customer and the store. Because customer could pay to the e-commerce, and then if they have recieved the goods, we can get our payment. If there is a negative feedback, it could affect the online store, whether being condemned or not getting any orders.
